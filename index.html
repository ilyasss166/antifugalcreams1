Understanding Antifungal Treatments

Antifungal creams are FDA-approved topical medications formulated to combat fungal infections by disrupting fungal cell membranes.

Proper application is crucial - most treatments require twice-daily application for 2-4 weeks.

Key Application Tips

  • Clean and dry affected area before application
  • Apply a thin layer and rub gently
  • Continue treatment for recommended duration
