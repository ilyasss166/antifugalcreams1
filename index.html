Understanding Antifungal Treatments

Antifungal creams are FDA-approved topical medications formulated to combat fungal infections by disrupting fungal cell membranes. Clinical studies show these treatments are 85-95% effective against common dermatophytes when used as directed.

Proper application is crucial - most treatments require twice-daily application for 2-4 weeks.
